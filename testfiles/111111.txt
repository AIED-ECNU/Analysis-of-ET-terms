                                  ZHANG Wei ，WANG Hai － rong 

                  ( Aopeng Distance Education Ｒesearch Institute ，Beijing ，100081，China) 

    Abstract : As a  new  model  of  online  education ，MOOC  have  got  the  widespread  concern  especially  in  High  Education  circles． 

There have been the MOOC platform in Chinese after joined several foreign well － known universities ，some universities are trying to ex- 

plore the blended model of MOOC and High Education． This paper based on the author ’s MOOC course learning and experience analy- 

sis ，from the  aspect  of  learners  to  analysis  the  course  idea ，Content  and  Form，evaluation  system ，management  and  organization ， 

course development ，and proposed the suggestion of MOOC development and indigenization in future． 

    Key words : MOOC ;  Courses ;  Learning experiences ;  Indigenization enlightenment
